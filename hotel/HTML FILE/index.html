Our Story

Where Leadership Meets Serenity

Nestled in the heart of New Takoradi at 2 Sir Charles Bucknor Street, Palena Gardens is more than a hotel — it is a living estate dedicated to the flourishing of individuals, teams, and organisations. Our seven elegantly appointed rooms sit alongside one of Takoradi's finest garden grounds, offering an environment of quiet beauty and restorative calm.

Through our Centre for Leadership, Wellbeing & Culture, we host executive retreats, coaching intensives, and cultural immersion programmes that help leaders and organisations discover their truest potential — rooted in African values and global best practice.

Centre for Leadership, Wellbeing & Culture

Where Leaders Are Shaped

Our Centre offers a unique ecosystem for leaders, organisations, and communities to explore, grow, and transform. Grounded in the cultural richness of Ghana's Western Region, our programmes blend global leadership frameworks with deep human wisdom.

Whether you're seeking an executive retreat, leadership coaching, a team offsite, or cultural immersion — Palena Gardens provides the space, expertise, and inspiration.

🧭
Leadership Retreats

Immersive multi-day programmes for senior leaders and executives. Strategy, self-leadership, and renewal.

🌿
Wellbeing Intensives

Evidence-based practices for mind-body-spirit wellness. Burnout recovery, resilience, and sustainable performance.

🥁
Culture & Identity

Rooted in Ghanaian heritage. Programmes exploring African leadership philosophy, cultural intelligence, and belonging.

🤝
Team Offsites

Facilitated team retreats for strategy alignment, culture building, and collective breakthrough.
